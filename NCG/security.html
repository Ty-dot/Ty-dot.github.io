How secure is your account?

Millions of people are hacked every year. These attacks can be for things as small as Domino's Pizza and Playstation Network accounts, to multi-million dollar Banking corporations and Cryptocurrency Wallets. Hackers often look for common passwords and use brute force methods to comb through thousands of accounts at once, hoping to find a loose end. Sometimes these things just happen out of chance, but we can do things such as choosing strong passwords to help try to prevent it.

Here are the most common passwords. Hopefully yours is not here... otherwise maybe you should change it!
